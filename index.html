DRAGON BALL SUPER: BROLY

Jalainie R. Abdullah Jr. | 9-Topaz

Forty-one years ago, King Vegeta discovered a baby named Broly with greater power than his son. He sent Broly to Vampa, hoping it would surpass Vegeta's. Broly's father, Paragus, attempted to rescue him, but his ship was damaged, leaving them trapped on Vampa. Years later, they discover salvation from an unlikely ally, who would eventually save them. Son Goku and Prince Vegeta, the last survivors of the Saiyan race, are training on a remote island when their old enemy, Frieza, appears. They search for the last Dragon Balls on a frozen continent, where Frieza shows off his new allies, Paragus and Broly. A legendary battle ensues as Goku and Vegeta face off against Broly, a warrior without equal, whose rage is just waiting to be unleashed.
